Aviation training, safety and technical analysis.

This website documents my development as a Portuguese aviation student preparing for integrated ATPL training, with a focus on procedure discipline, aviation safety, data-informed study and professional technical documentation.

Overview

Portfolio purpose

Tomás Santos Aviation is a professional portfolio created to organize aviation study, technical projects, written reports and training progress. The aim is to build a clear record of serious aviation development over time, supported by structured documentation and reliable public sources.

01

Pilot training

Preparation for integrated ATPL training, including aviation theory, procedure study, operational discipline and future training milestones.

02

Aviation safety

Articles and reports focused on safety-relevant topics such as runway incursions, unstable approaches, communication discipline and pilot decision-making.

03

Technical documentation

Use of Python, XeLaTeX and structured reporting methods to produce clean aviation analysis, references, charts and technical documents.

Work in progress

Initial reports and projects
